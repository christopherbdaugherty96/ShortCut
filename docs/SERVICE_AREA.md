# Service Area

## Primary Area
- Ypsilanti
- Belleville
- nearby surrounding neighborhoods

## Strategy
Focus on dense residential route growth instead of scattered long-distance jobs.

## Preferred Neighborhoods
- subdivisions
- HOA neighborhoods
- medium-size lawns
- visible lawn-maintenance areas

## Avoid Early
- large commercial properties
- extremely rural spread-out routes
- long-distance one-time jobs

## Route Goal
Build recurring route density with nearby customers clustered geographically.
