# Job Completion Checklist

## Before Starting
- Confirm address
- Confirm requested service
- Take before photos when useful
- Check for obstacles, pets, gates, and property hazards

## Service Steps
- Mow lawn evenly
- Trim around fences, beds, trees, posts, and edges
- Edge sidewalks and driveway when included
- Blow off sidewalks, driveway, porch, and walkways
- Check visible finish quality before leaving

## After Service
- Take after photos when useful
- Mark job complete
- Record notes about lawn condition
- Record any customer follow-up needed
- Send payment request or invoice if applicable

## Quality Standard
Leave the property looking cleaner than when the job started.

## Safety Rule
Do not perform work that exceeds current equipment, skill, insurance, or legal readiness.
