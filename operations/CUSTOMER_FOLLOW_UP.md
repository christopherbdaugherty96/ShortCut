# Customer Follow Up

## Goal
Turn one-time customers into recurring route customers.

## After First Service
Send a short follow-up message:

"Thank you for choosing ShortCut Lawn Care. We appreciate the opportunity to work on your property. Let us know if you would like recurring weekly or biweekly service going forward."

## Review Request
After successful jobs:
- ask for a review
- ask for referrals
- request before/after permission when appropriate

## Recurring Service Goal
Prioritize:
- weekly customers
- biweekly customers
- nearby repeat customers

## Important Rule
Consistent communication builds trust faster than aggressive sales tactics.
