# Starter Equipment Budget

## Basic Startup Equipment
### Push Mower or Commercial Mower
Estimated range:
$300–3000+

### String Trimmer
Estimated range:
$100–400

### Leaf Blower
Estimated range:
$100–400

### Fuel Containers
Estimated range:
$30–60

### Safety Gear
Estimated range:
$50–150

### Trailer (optional initially)
Estimated range:
$1000–4000+

## Important Rule
Do not overbuy equipment before recurring route density exists.

## Priority
Reliable equipment matters more than expensive branding early on.
