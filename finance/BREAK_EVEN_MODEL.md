# Break-Even Model

## Goal
Estimate how many recurring customers are needed before the business meaningfully covers operating costs.

## Common Expenses
- mower payments or maintenance
- trimmer and blower maintenance
- fuel
- trailer costs
- insurance
- business registration
- marketing materials
- equipment replacement

## Basic Principle
The first priority is covering recurring operating costs reliably.

## Operational Rule
Recurring customers stabilize the business.
One-time jobs help cash flow but should not be the foundation.

## Long-Term Goal
Build route density until recurring work reliably exceeds operational expenses.
